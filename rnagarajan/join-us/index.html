Join Ram Research Group

Why us?

Our research group offers a unique blend of academic excellence and industrial relevance. We're at the forefront of materials science and sustainable technology, with a strong focus on developing next-generation polymers and sustainable solutions. As a member of our team, you'll have access to state-of-the-art facilities, collaborate with leading industry partners, and contribute to groundbreaking research that addresses real-world challenges. We foster an environment where creativity meets scientific rigor, and where your individual growth is as important as our collective success.

Rigorous Scientific Training

  • Gain deep expertise in cutting-edge research methodologies
  • Develop creative problem-solving skills
  • Take ownership of impactful projects from conception to completion

Communication & Presentation Skills

  • Present research findings at group meetings and conferences
  • Author scientific manuscripts and reports
  • Engage with diverse audiences, from academic peers to industry stakeholders

Collaboration & Teamwork

  • Work in an interdisciplinary, fast-paced environment
  • Learn how to interact with and manage expectations of funding agencies
  • Collaborate with academic peers, industrial partners, and cross-functional teams
  • Develop healthy interdependence and teamwork skills

Industry-Ready Skills

  • Receive hands-on industrial training through collaborative projects
  • Build experience aligned with industry needs and expectations
  • Prepare to transition smoothly into industrial roles after graduation

Professional Development

  • Engage in proposal writing and grant management
  • Learn how to interact with and manage expectations of funding agencies
  • Provide monthly progress updates—building consistency and accountability
  • Work with minimal supervision while receiving necessary guidance

How to Apply

Please send your CV, a brief statement of research interests, and any relevant publications or projects to Ramaswamy_Nagarajan@uml.edu
